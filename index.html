Echoes of Arx Vitae

Atmospheric Puzzle Adventure

In the ruins of an ancient civilization, you play as Kira, a signal operator who discovers that the abandoned city of Arx Vitae still pulses with mysterious energy. As you restore communication networks throughout the city, you uncover the tragic story of its inhabitants and the choice that sealed their fate.

Navigate through hauntingly beautiful environments where every restored signal reveals fragments of memory, love, and loss. Your decisions will determine whether Arx Vitae's legacy becomes a warning or a beacon of hope for future generations.
